Canon Pixma TS3122 Wireless printer is compatible with Windows and Mac, so we will tell you how to step forward to connect your printer. Additionally, you will need to set up the printer hardware before you start the below process. Therefore, take all the components out from the Canon printer and assemble them as per the manual guidelines.

Connect your Pixma TS3122 Wireless printer to WiFi 

Ensure your TS3122printer is turned on, and the wireless network should also be active. If you see both of them with green lights, start with the below steps:-    

  • See whether the light is blinking. After that, tap on the “Stop” button. It will stop lights from blinking, which needs a restart.
  • Do you see a direct button on your ij.start.canon TS3122 model? Now you need to press the Direct button and hold it until you see the “Settings” option displaying on your printer model.
  • Once you tap on the Direct button, the printer will jump to Wireless Connect mode automatically. Here you should be able to see some of the icons.
  • You may or may not receive an installation CD with your Canon TS3122 printer if you have one, its great. You simply need to insert it into your PC CD-ROM. However, if you don’t get an installation CD, you have other options like visiting the Canon support site, i.e., ij.start.canon or canon.com/ijsetup and download the latest canon printer drivers for your Canon TS3122 printer model by clicking on “Setup” tab and entering your model name.
  • If you are using the software and drivers for the first, select the first computer name.
  • Once you make sure the Canon printer software is on your PC, you require installing it now. Hence, click on the “Start Setup” tab and continue with some shown instructions.
  • Thereafter, run the software and let it perform the task.
  • Choosing your country or region is essential, then hit Next. To continue the installation, you require taking a look at software terms and then accepting the license policy.
  • If you have mac device, then you will need to enter the mac login password to continue.
  • Now, see the WiFi network list shown on a new screen and select one with your ij.start.canon TS3122 connected. Thereafter, enter the password and go next.  
  • You will see the next window asking to select your printer from the given list. Then, select your TS3122 model and continue.
  • Now it shows you the next screen of running to complete the setup, which may take a few minutes. Hence wait for a while.
  • After the connection is made between your Canon printer and the system, the driver will take a few seconds to check for the problem.

If this doesn’t make you learn or created any errors during the above process, you should try software reinstallation, Canon TS3122 printer reset or contact the canon support team.
